## RBI KYC and Due Diligence Guidance

Regulated entities should apply enhanced due diligence when transaction structures,
counterparty identities, or documentary evidence create a heightened risk of fraud,
impersonation, or beneficial ownership concealment.

For supply chain finance workflows, lenders should verify:
- supplier and buyer identities,
- invoice authenticity,
- underlying purchase order and goods receipt evidence,
- abnormal transaction patterns across related entities.

Where the pattern of financing requests suggests duplicate financing, circular trade,
or unsupported receivable creation, the transaction should be escalated for review
before disbursement.
